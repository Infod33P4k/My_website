About Me

I’m a penetration tester and cybersecurity enthusiast with a strong passion for ethical hacking and cybersecurity. My journey in the field has equipped me with skills in vulnerability assessments, securing digital infrastructures, and creating automated tools for recon and testing. I have completed the Google Cybersecurity course on Coursera and achieved a ranking in the top 2% on TryHackMe, showcasing my commitment to staying ahead in the cybersecurity domain.

Additionally, I hold a CPT certification from the Red Team Hacker Academy in Chennai and am currently pursuing the prestigious CPTS certification from Hack The Box. These milestones reflect my dedication to continuous learning and applying advanced techniques to uncover vulnerabilities and enhance system security.

I believe in contributing to a safer digital world through innovation, collaboration, and relentless curiosity.
